Rules
First Lap

1. Four-sided two dice will be used.
2. Your points will be determined by the numbers on the dice.
3. If you get double, your point for that round will be doubled too!


Second Lap

1. A six-sided die will be used.
2. If you roll "1,2, or 4", you will get regular points.
3. If you roll "3", you lose 3 points.
4. If you roll "5", oil spills you lose 5 points.
5. If you roll "6", turbo boost you double and get 12 points.

Third Lap

1. A twelve-sided die will be used.
2. If you roll "2,4", you get regular points.
3. If you roll "1 or 6", you get double points.
4. If you roll "3", the computer will roll a eight-sided die.(This will be random and the computer will let you know what you get on events screen.)
5. If you roll "5", oil spills you lose 5 points.
6. If you roll "7", computer will roll a ten-sided die.(This will be random and the computer will let you know what you get on events screen.)
7. If you roll "8", accident you lose 10 points.
8. If you roll "9", ran into a town you lose 5 points.
9. If you roll "10", you get +15 points.
10. If you roll "11", burst tire you lose 8 points.
11. If you roll "12", the computer will roll a 20-sided die.(This will be random and the computer will let you know what you get on events screen.)
